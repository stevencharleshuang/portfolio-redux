About

I am a Web Developer with passions for music, gaming and all things related to problem-solving.

In 2018, I completed the 12-week intensive Web Development Immersive program at General Assembly, New York where we completed four major projects, three of which required full-stack development. It was during this time that I realized my passion for game development and have been unconventionally creating games using JavaScript with jQuery, Crafty.js and HTML 5 Canvas.

I graduated from the SUNY Purchase Conservatory of Music in 2009 with a Bachelor of Music in Studio Composition so when I'm not knee-deep in code, I enjoy composing, producing and recording music.
